Fully-Differential Folded-Cascode OpAmp (IBM 0.18μm CMR7SF)

A fully-differential folded-cascode operational amplifier was designed with IBM 0.18μm 7RF process. The amplifier has 85.3dB differential gain, cut-off frequency equal to 2.2MHz and differential output signal excursion greatear than 1V, operating with an 1.8V power supply. For more details refer to the technical report. This design was sent to fabrication and will be tested soon.
