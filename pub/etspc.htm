Design of high speed digital circuits with E-TSPC cell library

I made this project as my course conclusion work to obtain a Computer Engineering Degree at Universidade de Sao Paulo - Sao Carlos, later it was published in SBCCI 2011. Below you can find links to download the paper, the report (in Portuguese) and the design files. All design was made using Mentor Graphics tools and AMS 0.35μm Design Kit.
